The Opportunity

A sector at an inflection point

01 — The Problem

Mineral Discovery Rates Are Down

Traditional exploration is capital-intensive, slow, and high-risk — greenfield projects face just a 1-in-5,000 chance of commercial success, and average drill success hovers at only 0.5%. Meanwhile, surging demand and tightening supply for critical minerals intensify the need for transformative innovation.

02 — The Shift

AI Tools Unlock a $12T Opportunity

AI-driven platforms flip the equation — cutting discovery costs by up to 80% and boosting success rates to around 75%. This leap could deliver $290–$390 billion in annual industry savings by 2035. Hyperspectral sensing, quantum imaging, and digital twins make it possible to find deposits in previously inaccessible terrain.

03 — The Proof

Junior & Mid-Tier Miners Lead Adoption

More agile and open to innovation, junior and mid-tier miners are reaping the greatest rewards. Predictive targeting has already powered commercial discoveries like:

  • Mingomba Copper, Zambia — >3.5% Cu
  • Fontenoy Pd-Ni-Cu, NSW
  • Keats & Lotto Gold Zones, Newfoundland
  • Abbotts North Lithium, WA — up to 1.25% Li₂O
04 — The Frontier

AI + Remote Sensing = Next Frontier

As hyperspectral costs decline and commercial providers scale, AI-integrated sensing enables faster, more accurate detection of concealed deposits. The approach aligns with modern ESG expectations and reduces ground disturbance — streamlining permitting and accelerating time to discovery.
